## Section 4: Alcohol (Honest Section)

*This section is confidential and non-judgmental. Alcohol has a significant impact on fat loss, sleep, and recovery — I need to know the honest picture to give you a useful diagnosis.*

**Q13 — How would you describe your relationship with alcohol?** *(Multiple choice)*
- I don't drink (or very rarely — less than once a month)
- Light drinker — a few drinks occasionally
- Moderate — most weekends, sometimes weeknights
- Regular — most evenings, weekends, social occasions
- Heavy — daily or near-daily, 20+ units/week

**Q14 — Roughly how many units of alcohol do you drink per week?** *(Multiple choice)*
*1 unit = 1 small (125ml) glass of wine, half a pint of regular beer, or a single shot of spirits.*
- 0–2 units
- 3–7 units
- 8–14 units
- 15–20 units
- 21–28 units
- 28+ units

**Q15 — How do you feel about reducing alcohol as part of a reset?** *(Multiple choice)*
- I've already stopped / barely drink — not an issue
- I'd be willing to significantly reduce it
- I'd be willing to reduce it moderately — some social occasions are important to me
- I'd find it very hard — it's deeply social / habitual for me
- I'm not willing to change my drinking — be honest with me about whether Battleship can still work

## Notes for Claude (Intake Agent Processing)

When a completed form response comes in, paste the answers into the Intake Agent prompt (see `sub-agents/intake-agent.md`) to generate the diagnosis report. Key fields to extract:

- **main_goal**: from Q16
- **constraints**: from Q6 (schedule), Q9 (injuries), Q18 (time available)
- **risk_flags**: from Q7 (sleep), Q8 (stress), Q13/Q14 (alcohol), Q9 (health conditions)
- **level**: infer from Q10 + Q11 (first attempt = beginner; previous attempts + some fitness = restart; current exercise = already training)
- **history**: from Q11 + Q12 (what failed and why)
- **goal_description**: from Q19 (success definition in client's own words)
