# Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset (Final Vault – February 2026)

**Battleship** is the programme name and brand — my real middle name (chosen in my 30s), now the flagship for helping men 45–60 who've "failed before" reset their health.

**Core Niche Angle**  
“Simple, realistic system that works if you show up.”  
Focus: belly fat reduction, confidence, energy, blood markers, longevity.  
Reframe: “It failed before because the system didn’t fit your life or age, not because you’re weak.”

**Battleship Promise**  
Not a quick fix or plaster — a proper rewrite: simple steps, accountability, real tracking, adjustments. So you don’t become another statistic of early death or miserable midlife. You get to show up as the dad, husband, mate, and bloke you want to be — with energy, strength, zero regrets.

**Three Core Deliverables**  
1. **Intake & Diagnosis** → personalised “Why it failed + what will be different” report  
2. **12-Week Battleship Plan** → movement, workouts, nutrition, mindset  
3. **Ongoing Battleship Guidance & Tracking** → weekly check-ins, adjustments, education drips

**Multi-Agent Architecture**  
Orchestrator routes to sub-agents: Intake, Program, Check-in, Education.

- MyFitnessPal – tracking
