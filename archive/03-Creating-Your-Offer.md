# 3. Creating Your Offer – Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset

**Programme Name & Core Positioning**  
**Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset**  
A battle-tested, resilient system designed for men 45–60 who have been repeatedly sunk by past fitness and weight-loss attempts.  

The name comes from my own middle name (William George Battleship Barratt), chosen in my 30s because I liked what it stood for. Turns out I needed every bit of that resilience in my 40s — and now I'm passing the same framework on to other men in the same boat.

**Core Messaging**  
- “Simple, realistic system that works if you show up.”  
- Focus outcomes: visible belly fat reduction, restored confidence, daily energy, improved blood markers, longevity.  
- Reframe of past failures: “It failed before because the system didn’t fit your life or age — not because you’re weak or lack willpower.”  
- No extremes, no giving up social life — just winning the war against dead calories, low energy, regret, and early health risks.

**Product Ladder**  
1. Free Battleship Intake Quiz → personalised diagnosis report (automated via Intake Agent)  
2. Paid 12-week Battleship Programme (£150–250 one-time or £99/mo recurring) → full plan + onboarding  
3. Ongoing Battleship Membership (£79–99/mo after 12 weeks) → weekly check-ins, adjustments, education drips, accountability

**The Three Core Battleship Deliverables**

**Deliverable 1 – Intake & Diagnosis (Week 0)**  
- Long-form questionnaire covering:  
  - Past attempts & what the client believes failed  
  - Current schedule, injuries, equipment (home/gym/none)  
  - Sleep quality, stress, alcohol use/patterns  
  - Goals (belly fat / energy / confidence / blood markers / longevity)  
- Intake Agent processes form → tags main goal, constraints, risk flags  
- Output: 1-page personalised Battleship Diagnosis Report  
  - “Why it failed so far” (non-shaming, system-focused)  
  - “What will be different this time” (clear, realistic path forward)

**Deliverable 2 – 12-Week Battleship Plan (Core Transformation Phase)**  
Generated by Program Agent using client tags, fitness level (beginner / restart / already training), and preferences.

**Movement (Non-negotiable foundation)**  
- Daily minimum 10,000 steps — brisk walking in Zone 2 (110–120 bpm, ~5 km)  
- Why Zone 2? Maximises aerobic fat-burning, improves mitochondrial health & insulin sensitivity, very low injury risk (unlike HIIT which can wreck ageing knees/joints).  
- Goal: build NEAT & cardiovascular base without burnout.

**Workouts (3–4 sessions per week)**  
- 30–50 minutes including warm-up  
- Bodyweight + basic weights (dumbbells, pull-up bar, resistance bands, or gym access)  
- 3–4 main movements per session (e.g. push-ups / squats / rows / deadlift pattern / overhead press)  
- Auto-progression rules: increase reps/weight/sets when target is comfortably hit (e.g. +5–10% load when 3×12 achieved)  
- Track progress in app or notebook; report for feedback & next-week adjustment

**Nutrition (3–5 simple, sustainable rules)**  
- High protein target (1.6–2.2 g/kg bodyweight)  
- Calorie deficit if high body fat % (tracked via MyFitnessPal) → shift to maintenance/slight surplus as lean mass increases  
- Avoid/minimise: processed foods, seed oils, excess alcohol (dead calories & recovery killer)  
- Focus: whole foods, self-prepped meals, consistent eating windows  
- Weekly alcohol guidance: track units accurately; significant reduction (or removal) usually required for optimal fat loss & energy gains

**Mindset & Habits**  
- Patience & consistency over perfection  
- Track everything (steps, food, lifts, energy, waist)  
- Redefine success: energy & strength gains > scale number  
- Face discomfort head-on (normal part of being a dad/husband/bloke)

**Deliverable 3 – Ongoing Battleship Guidance & Tracking (Month 4+)**  
- **Three pillars**: Measure / Move / Food  
- **Client investment**:  
  - Time commitment (daily logging + weekly check-in)  
  - Basic tools: decent walking shoes, all-weather gear, fitness tracker (mandatory), optional home weights/pull-up bar  
- **Daily logging** (MyFitnessPal or similar):  
  - Food (calories/macros — critical for seeing dead-calorie impact)  
  - Weight & waist measurement  
  - Exercise (steps, workout volume)  
  - Energy/mood score (1–10)  
- **Weekly Check-in** (Check-in Agent):  
  - Review logs → praise wins, one key correction, next week’s focus  
  - Adjust volume, exercise selection, nutrition rules (e.g. “add 10 min post-dinner walk”)  
- **Education Agent** drips bite-sized lessons:  
  - Why diets failed before  
  - All-or-nothing thinking trap  
  - Sleep, NEAT, protein & strength for longevity  
  - Realistic alcohol impact (dead calories, fat-burning shutdown, recovery hit)

**Agent Responsibilities Summary**  
- **Intake Agent** → tags data & generates Battleship diagnosis report  
- **Program Agent** → builds/adapts the 12-week Battleship plan  
- **Check-in Agent** → analyses weekly logs, adjusts plan, writes coach message  
- **Education Agent** → selects & drips relevant lessons
