# 2. Essential Setup – Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset

**Goal**  
Get the legal, financial, technical, and operational foundations in place quickly and cheaply so you can focus on delivering value and acquiring clients — not admin.

**Estimated time**  
4–8 hours spread over 1–2 days (most can be done in one focused afternoon).

**Order of operations** (do in this sequence)

### 1. Legal & Professional Basics (UK-specific)
- **Business name**: Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset (or simply "Battleship Fitness" / "Battleship Coaching").  
  - Check name availability: Companies House (free search) + domain registrar.  
  - Register as sole trader (simplest & cheapest for starting):  
    - HMRC online → register for Self Assessment (takes ~10 minutes).  
    - No need to form a limited company until £30k–50k+ revenue.
- **Public Liability Insurance** (essential for any coaching involving movement advice):  
  - PolicyBee, Simply Business, or Insure4Sport.  
  - Aim for £1–2 million cover (~£80–150/year).  
  - Mention online coaching + exercise guidance (not personal training in-person).  
- **Basic legal documents** (use templates, customise lightly):  
  - Terms & Conditions  
  - Privacy Policy (GDPR compliant)  
  - Client waiver/disclaimer (health not medical advice, consult doctor if needed)  
  - Sources: Rocket Lawyer UK, LawDepot, or Pulse-style fitness templates.

### 2. Payments & Money
- **Stripe account** (primary processor):  
  - Sign up at stripe.com/gb (UK-friendly).  
  - Verify identity (passport/driving licence + proof of address).  
  - Create products:  
    - 12-week Battleship Programme (£199 one-time or 3×£75)  
    - Ongoing Battleship Membership (£89/mo)  
  - Generate payment links → embed in emails/landing pages.
- **Accounting & tracking**:  
  - Free: Wave or Countingup (UK bank + bookkeeping).  
  - Paid (recommended once revenue starts): Xero or QuickBooks (~£10–25/mo).  
  - Track every expense (ads, insurance, tools) from day 1 for tax relief.

### 3. Core Tech Stack Setup
- **Domain & basic website**:  
  - Buy battleshipreset.com / battleshipfitness.co.uk / willbattleship.com (~£10–15/year via Namecheap or 123-reg).  
  - Simple landing page: Carrd.co (£19/year) or Leadpages.  
    - Headline: "Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset"  
    - Sub: "Simple system for men 45–60 who've failed before."  
    - Prominent intake quiz button.
- **Email & CRM**:  
  - Pulse (fitness-specific, has templates for challenges/onboarding) or ActiveCampaign / ConvertKit.  
  - Set up: welcome sequence + diagnosis report delivery.
- **Client tracking tools**:  
  - MyFitnessPal (clients use free app for food/steps logging).  
  - Google Forms / Typeform → intake quiz (free tier fine to start).  
  - Apple Health / Google Fit export (optional for steps data).
- **Private community**:  
  - Facebook Group (free, best for 45–60 UK men).  
    - Name: "Battleship Crew – Midlife Fitness Reset"  
    - Private, pinned welcome post + rules + weekly check-in thread.

### 4. Personal & Branding Essentials
- **Profile photo & bio**:  
  - Recent photo (smiling, outdoors if possible — shows energy).  
  - Bio (X, LinkedIn, FB, website):  
    "William Battleship Barratt | Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset  
    Helping men 45–60 drop belly fat, reclaim energy & confidence with a system that actually fits real life.  
    Ex-stubborn drinker turned 17–18 fitness age at 47. No hype, just results."
- **Content tools** (quick & cheap):  
  - Phone + tripod for vertical videos (Zone 2 walks, push-up demos).  
  - CapCut or iMovie for simple edits.  
  - Canva for graphics (quotes, before/after templates).
