# 5. Getting Clients – Battleship – Midlife Fitness Reset

**Objective**  
Acquire the first 5–10 paying Battleship clients quickly (weeks 1–8), then scale to 1–5 new clients/week consistently.  
Focus: men 45–60 who've "failed before" — busy professionals, dads, husbands who want belly fat gone, energy back, confidence up, without extremes or giving up social life.

**Core Messaging Reminder**  
- "It failed before because the system didn’t fit your life or age — not because you’re weak."  
- "Battleship: simple, realistic system that works if you show up."  
- No hype, no six-pack promises — just energy, strength, better blood pressure, hobbies revived.

**Primary Channels & Tactics (ranked by expected ROI for this audience in 2026)**

1. **Facebook Ads (Primary Paid Channel – Highest ROI for 45–60 men)**  
   - Platform: Meta Ads Manager (run Facebook + Instagram in one campaign).  
   - Why Facebook first: highest daily active users in 45–65 UK male demographic, excellent for lead forms, retargeting, private Groups, interest targeting ("men's health over 40", "weight loss after 45", "blood pressure monitor", "Apple Watch", "longevity").  
   - Instagram as secondary: use for visual reels (Zone 2 walks, push-up progress, before/after waist shots) but expect lower conversion from this age group.  
   - Ad types to test:  
     - Lead form ads → direct to intake quiz  
     - Video ads (you walking/talking: "I was 55 fitness age at 47… here's what changed")  
     - Carousel ads (problem → diagnosis → simple fix → CTA)  
   - Budget start: £10–30/day → scale when cost-per-lead < £10–15 and quiz-to-paid conversion > 10%.  
   - Targeting: age 45–65, men, UK, interests (fitness, men's health, weight loss, hypertension, Apple Health), lookalikes from quiz completers.  
   - Creative tip: Use your real story/photos — authenticity beats polished ads for this demographic.

2. **Organic Facebook & Community Building**  
   - Private FB Group: "Battleship Crew – Midlife Fitness Reset" (invite-only feel).  
     - Post daily: quick tips (Zone 2 explanation, alcohol tracking reality, mindset reframes).  
     - Share mini-wins from your journey or early clients (anonymised).  
     - Run weekly "Battleship Check-in" threads.  
   - Join & contribute to relevant public groups: "Men's Health UK", "Fitness Over 40", "Dad Fitness", "Midlife Crisis No More".  
     - Don't spam — give value first (answer questions, share non-sales tips).

3. **X/Twitter (Organic + Light Paid)**  
   - Post threads: "Why most men 45–60 fail at weight loss (and the simple system that finally worked for me)"  
   - Short videos: 30–60s clips of walks, chin-ups, MyFitnessPal logging.  
   - Use hashtags sparingly: #MidlifeFitness #MensHealthUK #BellyFatOver40  
   - Engage: reply to men posting about stalled progress, low energy, hypertension — offer value, soft invite to quiz.  
   - Optional: X ads (£5–10/day) for lead gen if organic traction builds.

4. **Outbound / Direct Outreach (Fast First Clients)**  
   - "Clients on a Plate" style: search X, Reddit, FB groups for men venting about failed diets, low energy, midlife weight gain.  
   - DM/message template (warm, non-salesy):  
     "Saw your post about [specific struggle]. I was exactly there at 47 — fitness age 55, hypertension, regret. Built a simple system that finally worked without extremes. Happy to share the free Battleship intake quiz if you're curious?"  
   - Goal: 5–10 conversations → 1–2 intake quizzes per day of outreach.

5. **Content Engine to Attract & Nurture**  
   - Weekly output: 5+ pieces (mix of X threads, FB posts, short videos).  
   - Topics that convert well for this audience:  
     - "Why Zone 2 walking beats HIIT after 45"  
     - "Alcohol: the hidden dead-calorie torpedo sinking midlife progress"  
     - "I added it as my middle name for a reason — here's how Battleship got me from fat & frail to fit at 47"  
     - "The one thing I wish I'd known before my 40th: tracking beats willpower"  
   - CTA in every piece: link to Battleship intake quiz.

6. **Referral & Testimonials Loop**  
   - After first 3–5 clients: ask for short video/text testimonial (waist measurement drop, energy score up, BP improved).  
   - Feature in ads/content: "John, 53: -18 cm waist, fitness age down 12 years — no gym bro stuff."  
   - Referral incentive: "Refer a mate who joins → both get an extra month free."

**Success Milestones**  
- Week 1–2: 20–50 quiz completions (mostly organic + small ad test)  
- Week 3–4: First 1–3 paid clients  
- Month 2+: 1–5 new clients/week, CPL < £15, conversion quiz-to-paid > 10%
