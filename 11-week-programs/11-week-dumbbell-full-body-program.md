### 1. 11-Week Dumbbell Full-Body Program

**Frequency:** 3× per week  
**Focus:** Compound movements hitting all major muscle groups  
**Equipment:** Pair of dumbbells (adjustable ideal; increase weight when you hit upper rep range easily)

**Exercises (perform in this order each session):**
- Goblet Squat
- Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
- Dumbbell Bench Press (or floor press)
- Bent-Over Dumbbell Row
- Overhead Press
- Reverse Lunge (per leg)
- Plank (core finisher)

**Progression Blocks:**
- Weeks 1–3: 3 sets × 8–12 reps
- Weeks 4–6: 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps (add set when ready)
- Weeks 7–9: 4 sets × 6–10 reps (increase weight, focus strength)
- Weeks 10–11: 4 sets × 6–10 reps + intensity technique on final set (e.g., drop set, rest-pause, or slow eccentrics)
