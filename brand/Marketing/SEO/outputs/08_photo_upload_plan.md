### 2. 🟦 Cover Photo
**→ Use: `random-snaps/IMG_0448.jpeg`**
*(Cliff path lifestyle)*

**Why:** The cover photo is your billboard. A cliff path shot communicates movement, freedom, outdoor lifestyle, and progression — all core Battleship Reset brand values. It's visually dramatic without requiring gym equipment or a staged shoot. Men in the 40–60 bracket will emotionally connect with "that could be me on that walk."

**Editing Notes:**
- Crop to **16:9 ratio (1080×608px minimum)**
- Will should ideally sit in the **left or centre third** — GBP crops the edges on mobile
- Check the horizon is level — straighten if needed
- Keep the sky/path visible — do not centre-crop too tightly on the figure
- **Do not add text** — Google may reject or suppress text-heavy cover images
- Target export: **1080×608px, JPG, under 5MB**

> ⚠️ **Mobile preview warning:** GBP cover photos are cropped differently on mobile vs desktop. After uploading, check both views and re-upload with adjusted crop if Will's figure is cut off.

### 3. 🔴 Before/After — Transformation Composite
**→ Use: `brand/output/before_after_hook_02.jpg`**
*(Composite with hook text)*

**Why:** This is your highest-conversion asset. A purpose-built composite with hook text already does the storytelling work — no guesswork for the viewer. The "2 stone, walking only, 18 months" story is extraordinary and this image should carry it.

**Editing Notes:**
- Verify the composite includes `2024-pool-pic.jpg` as the "before" — if it doesn't, rebuild it so the contrast is stark and honest
- Check that hook text is **legible at thumbnail size** (GBP shows photos small in the grid)
- Ensure image is **at least 1200×900px (4:3)** for best GBP display
- If text is very dense, consider a **cleaner version with minimal text** for GBP specifically (save the full hook version for social)
- Target export: **1200×900px, JPG, under 5MB**

> ⚠️ **GBP text policy:** Google discourages promotional text in photos and may reduce visibility of heavily text-based images. If the hook text is large/bold, create a **secondary version with just the composite and minimal or no text** for GBP upload. Use the full text version on Instagram/Facebook instead.
