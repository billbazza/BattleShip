# How to Use Claude GBP Bot

**Claude Cowork doesn't rank your business. You do.**  

What it does is eliminate every excuse:  
- "I don't have time to analyze competitors." → Done in minutes.  
- "I don't know what categories/attributes to add." → Done.  
- "I don't know what to post/respond/upload." → Done.

The strategy still requires a human brain:  
- Knowing which keywords actually bring revenue.  
- Understanding your local market nuances.  
- Reading between the lines (e.g., competitor backlinks from local sponsorships, not just SEO).  
- Making final decisions on changes.

Claude does the heavy research fast. You apply the insight.

## Recommended Rollout Order (don't run all at once)

**Week 1 — Fix the foundation**  
- 1. GBP Category Audit  
- 2. GBP Attributes Audit  
→ Fastest fixes, biggest immediate ranking impact. Changes can show in days.

**Week 2 — Optimize your listing content**  
- 6. Services Section Optimization  
- 7. GBP Description Optimization  
→ Fills your profile with keyword-rich, Google-indexable text.

**Week 3 — Review engine**  
- 3. Competitor Review Teardown  
- 4. Review Response Strategy  
→ Sets your review velocity target + turns every review into a ranking + trust signal.

**Week 4 — Ongoing content & activity**  
- 5. GBP Posts Strategy  
- 8. GBP Photo Audit  
→ Builds the "active business" signal + location relevance week after week.

**Week 5 and beyond — Execute & compound**  
- Post 2–3× per week  
- Upload 3–5 quality photos weekly  
- Respond to every review (using templates)  
- Monitor & iterate (test description versions, add categories as needed)

This sequence compounds: foundation → content → signals → momentum.
