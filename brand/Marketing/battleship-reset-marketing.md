# Battleship Reset – Marketing & Brand Bible
Last updated: March 2025 (for ongoing use in brand bot / Claude / copy generation)

## Core Brand Identity
- **Target audience**: Men 40–55 (primarily UK), busy professionals / dads / tech-minded guys who used to be active but life got in the way. Done with excuses, want results without extreme gym-bro culture or crash diets.
- **Tone / Voice**: Direct, no-BS, mate-in-the-pub, systems-engineer explaining to another systems guy. Warm but firm. Zero bro-science, zero motivational fluff. Sarcastic humour allowed in small doses. Use British spelling (realise, programme, personalised).
- **Core metaphor**: Battleship – slow, heavy, unstoppable once moving in the right direction. Not a speedboat. Not flashy. Reliable, built for long voyages.
- **Positioning**: The anti-guru, anti-template, anti-“just do my 12-week programme blindly” coaching business. Personalised to the man’s actual life (injuries, shift work, kids, 60-hour weeks, love of beer).
- **Price anchor**: £199 for 12 weeks = roughly £16–17/week = “cost of one night out a week”.

## Primary USP Block (Hero / Ads / Emails / Landing page – use this verbatim or very close)
### Battleship Reset: The No-Excellence, Real-Life Reset for Men Over 40

**For the cost of ONE night out a week** (£16–17), you get the complete system most guys waste years and thousands chasing:

- **100% personalised training & nutrition plan** — no templates, no cookie-cutter PDFs. Built from your exact intake answers (injuries, work hours, family commitments, gym access — everything).
- **No two programmes are ever the same** — because no two lives are the same.
- **Weekly personalised review + adjustments** — I read your check-in, reply like a mate who actually gives a shit, and tweak the plan so you never stall.
- **Built-in accountability** — someone in your corner every single week for 12 weeks. No ghosting, no “just log it in the app and disappear”.
- **Science-based lessons delivered to your inbox** — Zone 2 training, protein timing, sleep hacks, fat-loss math, alcohol strategy — stuff that actually sticks because it’s explained like a systems guy to another systems guy.
- **Starts stupidly simple** — we begin with walking (yes, 20 km/day like I did) so you build the habit before you ever touch a barbell. No 5 am bootcamps, no crash diets.
- **7-day money-back guarantee** — if it doesn’t feel right, you’re out. No questions, no hard feelings.

You’re not buying workouts.  
You’re buying a battleship that doesn’t drift — steady, unstoppable, built around your actual life.

## Facebook / Meta Ad Creative Formulas (proven structure)
1. **Before/After + Big Claim**
   Visual: Your fat → thin photos side-by-side  
   Headline: “I went from 47 and knackered to fitness age 17… here’s exactly how”  
   Body: 2–3 lines of USP bullets → “Free 4-min diagnosis” CTA

2. **Pain → Solution Carousel**
   Slide 1: “Still making excuses at 45+?” (list pains)  
   Slide 2–5: One USP bullet per slide with icon  
   Slide 6: Your photo + “Cost of one night out a week”  
   Slide 7: “Start here →” (intake link)

3. **Story Video (15–30 sec phone video)**
   “Nine months ago I was 20 stone and couldn’t walk up stairs without stopping. Now I’m doing 20 km walks and feel 17 again. No gym. No PT. Just a stupidly simple system that actually fits real life. Want the exact blueprint? Click below.”

##禁止 / Avoid These Tropes
- Gym-bro flexing / “shred season” language
- “Transform in 12 weeks!” hype
- Before/afters without context (always tie to story)
- Generic motivational quotes
- Over-promising (stick to realistic, slow, sustainable)
