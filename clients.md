## Pipeline Stages

1. **Cold** — Saw content / ad, no action taken
2. **Warm** — Engaged (liked, commented, DM'd, or clicked ad)
3. **Intake Quiz Completed** — Submitted long-form intake form
4. **Diagnosis Sent** — Received personalised Battleship Diagnosis Report
5. **Enrolled** — Paid and onboarded (programme started)
6. **Active Member** — Ongoing monthly membership (post-12 weeks)
7. **Churned** — Left the programme (log reason)
