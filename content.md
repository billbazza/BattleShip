## Content Strategy Reference

**Platforms (priority order):**
1. Facebook (primary — best for men 45–60 UK)
2. X / Twitter (authority building, threads)
3. Instagram (secondary — visual proof, reels)
4. YouTube Shorts (Zone 2 walk clips, strength demos)

**Content Pillars (rotate across these):**

1. **Personal Story** — Will's transformation, the pool photo, fitness age 17–18, real moments
2. **System vs. Willpower** — "It failed before because the system was wrong, not you"
3. **Simple Wins** — Zone 2 walking, progressive overload, 10k steps, protein habits
4. **Alcohol Honest** — Dead calories, insulin impact, recovery hit — no judgment, just facts
5. **Men 45–60 Specific** — Knees, desk jobs, energy crashes, bad sleep, family pressure
6. **Social Proof** — Client wins (anonymised until real testimonials exist; use mock client for now)
7. **Education** — Bite-sized lessons: fasting basics, visceral fat, Zone 2 science, sleep

**Content Formats:**
- **Short text post** (FB/X): 100–200 words, hook + story + CTA
- **Thread** (X): 8–12 tweet thread on one topic (e.g. "Why Zone 2 walking beats HIIT for men over 45")
- **Short video** (30–90 sec): Zone 2 walk clips, push-up demo, talking head "one thing I learned"
- **Carousel/graphic** (FB/IG): Before/after stats, quote cards, "3 reasons why..."
- **Long-form post** (FB): 300–500 words personal story or lesson (best for Facebook algorithm)

**Posting Schedule Target:**
- Mon: Personal story or transformation post (FB + X)
- Tue: Education post (Zone 2, fasting, visceral fat) (FB + X)
- Wed: "System vs willpower" reframe post (FB + X)
- Thu: Short video or carousel (IG + FB)
- Fri: Alcohol / real life honest post (FB + X)
- Weekend: Engagement in FB groups, reply to comments, DMs
